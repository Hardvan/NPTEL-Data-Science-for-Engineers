Find R² and adjust R² for the following data:  
Given: Y = 0.2X + 60

| X   | Y   |
| --- | --- |
| 80  | 85  |
| 75  | 70  |
| 85  | 80  |
| 70  | 95  |
| 65  | 70  |

Solution:

Mean of Y = 80

| X   | Y   | Predicted Y | (Y - Mean of Y)² | (Predicted Y - Actual Y)² | (Predicted Y - Mean of Y)² |
| --- | --- | ----------- | ---------------- | ------------------------- | -------------------------- |
| 80  | 85  | 76          | 25               | 81                        | 16                         |
| 75  | 70  | 75          | 100              | 25                        | 25                         |
| 85  | 80  | 77          | 0                | 9                         | 9                          |
| 70  | 95  | 74          | 225              | 441                       | 36                         |
| 65  | 70  | 73          | 100              | 9                         | 49                         |

SST = 25 + 100 + 0 + 225 + 100 = 450  
SSE = 81 + 25 + 9 + 441 + 9 = 565  
SSR = 16 + 25 + 9 + 36 + 49 = 135
